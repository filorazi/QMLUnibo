About the Group

The Quantum Machine Learning Group at the University of Bologna is a research collective dedicated to exploring the intersection of quantum computing and machine learning. Our mission is to advance the field by developing novel algorithms and computational models that leverage the power of quantum mechanics to enhance data analysis, optimization, and artificial intelligence.

Our Members

The members of our group tackle the problems of quantum machine learning from a Computer Science per spective, with backround in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Big Data, Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining.
